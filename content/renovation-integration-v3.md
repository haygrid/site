# The best time to design your system is before renovation begins

Most technology problems start before anything is installed.

When systems are added after renovation, they are forced to fit into a space that wasn't designed for them — leading to compromises, visible wiring, and suboptimal performance.

We work with you early — during planning and renovation — so your system is designed into your space from the start.

## What happens when systems are added too late

When technology is treated as an afterthought, the space ends up shaping the system rather than the other way around. Cables are routed wherever they fit, equipment goes wherever there's room, and coverage gaps appear in exactly the areas that matter most. Fixing these issues after the fact is possible — but it comes with higher cost, additional disruption, and compromises that are often permanent.

Once walls are closed and layouts are fixed, your options become limited. The decisions that are easiest to get right before renovation are the hardest and most expensive to undo after.


---

## A design-first approach

We plan your entire system — network infrastructure, cable routing, device placement, and coverage — before renovation begins. Your physical space and your technology are designed together, not one after the other.

Everything is built on the Haygrid Stack, so the components we specify are already proven to work together. By the time your renovation is complete, your system is ready.


---

## Working alongside your renovation team

We coordinate directly with your renovation or interior design team — aligning on layout plans, specifying cable routes before walls are closed, and ensuring every device is placed exactly where it needs to be.

This isn't a handoff. We stay involved through the renovation to make sure the system is implemented exactly as designed — so nothing needs to be reworked after the fact.
